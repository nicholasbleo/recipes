Spaghetti with Garlic and Oil

Description

A classic fresh and spicy Italian spaghetti recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of spaghetti
  • 1 cup of chopped parsely
  • 1/3 cup of olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes
  • 3-6 cloves of sliced garlic
  • 1/4 cup of freshly ground parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water with 1 tbsp of salt.
  2. Heat the olive oil and red pepper flakes on medium-low heat.
  3. Cook the garlic until it just barely changes color.
  4. Boil the pasta until al dente (usually 1-2 minutes less than the package time).
  5. Toss the pasta in the oil with cheese and parsley.

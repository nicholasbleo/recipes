Roasted Rosemary Chicken

Description

A simple and low-maintenance Italian style chicken roasting recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (~3 lbs)
  • 3-6 cloves of garlic
  • 2 sprigs of rosemary
  • Salt & pepper
  • Light olive oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Rinse and dry the chicken, and get rid of any guts in the cavity.
  3. Cover with light olive oil, and add salt and pepper to taste (in the cavity, too).
  4. Fill the cavity of the chicken with the garlic cloves and one roesmary sprig.
  5. Pull the leaves off of the other sprig and sprinkle them on the chicken.
  6. Put the chicken in the middle of the oven, in a roasting pan, breast side down.
  7. Turn the chicken over once every 30 minutes, basting with the oil in the pan. The total cook time is about 30 minutes per pound.

Sage Butter Gnocchi

Description

A hearty and refreshing pasta recipe that's fun to make with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of small boiler potatoes
  • Flour
  • 1 egg
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 juiced lemon
  • 8 sage leaves

Steps

  1. Peel, boil and smash the potatoes, preferably with a ricer.
  2. Mix flour and one egg into the potatoes until the dough is soft but keeps its shape, and is not too sticky.
  3. Roll the dough into rolls about 1/2 inch thick, cut into small pieces, and press & roll them on the back of a fork.
  4. Boil the gnocchi in salted water until floating, then drain and cover to keep warm.
  5. Melt the butter on medium-low heat until it is just slighly darkened.
  6. Add sage leaves and remove from heat after about 30 seconds.
  7. Add the lemon juice.
  8. Toss the gnocci in the sauce with the grated cheese.
